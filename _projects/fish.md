> *You can tune a piano, but you can't tuna fish*

The **fish** tuner is a web-based tuner. You can use it at [naitian.org/fish](https://naitian.org/fish).

**fish** can stand for:

- **F**oreman **is** **h**appy
- **F**lat **i**sn't **sh**arp
- **Fi**nd **s**ome **h**armony
